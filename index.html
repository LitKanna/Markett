Quick questions

Where exactly do I pick up?

Paddy's Markets Flemington, Building D at Sydney Markets, 250 Parramatta Road, Flemington NSW 2129. We send the exact meeting spot with your confirmation.

How do I pay?

Cash or card on pickup, or pay online when you book. Prepaid orders get priority: they're packed first and guaranteed even on sold-out weeks.

What eggs are they?

Pace Farm eggs in sealed, stamped 30-egg trays (1.75kg). The same brand sold in major stores, at a better price.

Can I order in bulk for my café or stall?

Yes. Choose "Full box" and set how many you need, up to 10 boxes (1,800 eggs). Weekly business orders get their stock put aside first.
