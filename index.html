Need to know

Quick answers.

Where do I pick up?

Building D at Paddy's Markets Flemington (Sydney Markets, 250 Parramatta Rd, NSW 2129). We text you the exact spot and time with your confirmation.

How do I pay?

Cash or card at pickup, or pay online when you book. Prepaid orders are packed first, so they're locked in even on busy weeks.

What eggs are they?

Pace Farm cage eggs, 30 to a tray (1.75kg). The same brand you'll find in the big supermarkets, for less.

Can I order in bulk?

Yes. Pick "Full box" and choose how many, up to 10 boxes (1,800 eggs). Cafés and stalls on a weekly order get their stock set aside first.
