# YOLKO Design System — locked (v126)

**Design read:** Local-food conversion landing for Flemington shoppers — honest market brand with modern DTC clarity. Forest ink + yolk amber on cool stone. Not Pace Farm chrome. Not cream craft. Not purple AI.

**Dials:** VARIANCE 6 · MOTION 3 · DENSITY 4

**Must follow:** `docs/MODERN-WEB-TASTE-GUIDE.md` + `.cursor/skills/modern-web-taste/SKILL.md`

## One job
Book a Pace Farm tray for Friday or Saturday pickup at Flemington.

## Brand values (lead with these)
1. **Fair price** — market-direct trays at $12, not supermarket markup
2. **Book ahead** — reserve online so you don’t miss out
3. **Weekend-fresh** — Friday & Saturday pickup only, when you need them
4. **Real Pace Farm** — large eggs, 1.75kg trays, sealed for pickup

## Signature (only one)
Higgsfield open studio tray as a designed hero mark on exact page stone (`--stone` `#E2E4DE`). Soft-dissolved edges. Brand wordmark lives in the nav at hero scale of attention.

## Hero copy (locked)
```
Flemington market mornings
Fresh eggs. Fair price.
$12 · 30 large · Pace Farm · 1.75kg
Book online. Collect Friday or Saturday at Paddy's Markets.
```

## Tray rules (hard)
1. Page `--stone` `#E2E4DE` matches Higgsfield hero ground.
2. Hero tray = designed mark, not a photo column / card / frame.
3. Closed lid only in order section.
4. No border-radius, gray fill, box-shadow, or drop-shadow on the tray.
5. Never rembg the open hero tray.
