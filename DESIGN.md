# YOLKO Design Plan — Industry craft pass (v104)

**Design read:** Local-food conversion landing for Flemington shoppers. Personality kept (market warmth), craft fixed to industry standards.

**Dials:** VARIANCE 5 · MOTION 3 · DENSITY 3

## What was wrong in v103 (audit)
1. Two competing #1s (EGGS. vs giant $12)
2. Three accents (leaf + yolk + stamp red)
3. Hero clutter (stamps + disc + tray + ticket)
4. Mobile: product before headline/CTA (CTA below fold)
5. Comic 2px borders + hard offset shadows
6. Decorative marquee fighting conversion
7. Pricing cards without bottom-aligned CTAs
8. Brand only in nav — failed brand-first / brand-test

## Standards applied (2026 landing checklist)
- Full-bleed hero product plane (no inset media card, no overlays)
- Brand-first: giant **YOLKO** is the hero signal; headline supports
- Hero budget: brand + one headline + one lede + one CTA group
- One primary accent (leaf) for CTAs; yolk only for brand energy / list dots
- Body ≥ 16px; touch targets ≥ 44–48px
- Soft 12px button radius (not pill clusters)
- Pricing cards only where interaction lives (choose + book); CTAs bottom-aligned
- Pickup days as a list, not decorative cards
- FAQ as rules, not boxed cards
- Focus rings, reduced motion, WebP < 200KB
- Sentence-case headers
