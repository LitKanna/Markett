# YOLKO Design System — locked (v129)

**Design read:** Local-food conversion landing for Flemington shoppers — honest market brand with modern DTC clarity. Forest ink + yolk amber on cool stone. Premium = air, not density. Not Pace Farm chrome. Not cream craft. Not purple AI.

**Dials:** VARIANCE 5 · MOTION 3 · DENSITY 3

**Must follow:** `docs/MODERN-WEB-TASTE-GUIDE.md` + `.cursor/skills/modern-web-taste/SKILL.md`

## One job
Book a Pace Farm tray for Friday or Saturday pickup at Flemington.

## Brand values
1. **Fair price** — market-direct trays at $12
2. **Book ahead** — reserve online so you don’t miss out
3. **Weekend-fresh** — Friday & Saturday pickup
4. **Real Pace Farm** — large eggs, 1.75kg trays

## Signature
Higgsfield open studio tray as a designed hero mark on `--stone` `#E2E4DE` — subtle muted looping video (`assets/tray-hero.webm` / `.mp4`) with static poster fallback. Soft-dissolved edges. Brand in nav.

## Hero copy (locked)
```
Flemington · Fri & Sat
Fresh eggs.
Fair price.
$12 · 30 large · Pace Farm
Book online. Collect at Paddy's Markets Flemington.
[Book your tray]
```
Premium rules: intentional 2-line headline, generous vertical rhythm, one CTA, weight 700 not 800, no cramped stacks.

## Tray rules
1. Page stone matches tray ground.
2. Hero tray = designed mark, not a photo frame.
3. Closed lid only in order section.
4. No radius / fill / shadow on the tray.
5. Never rembg the open hero tray.
