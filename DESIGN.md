# YOLKO Design Plan — Modern creative (v105)

**Design read:** Local-food conversion landing for Flemington shoppers, modern DTC creative language (Allbirds / Glossier clarity + market product proof), not craft-cream editorial.

**Dials:** VARIANCE 8 · MOTION 6 · DENSITY 3

## Why v104 failed
- Warm cream + Fraunces = AI craft default
- Full-bleed dark scrim over busy label photo = dated overlay hero
- Leaf-green craft CTAs + yolk brand fight
- Felt like a farm brochure, not a 2026 consumer site

## Direction
- **Cool stone** ground (not cream paper)
- **Ink** primary CTAs (modern DTC), yolk only for price energy
- **Syne + Outfit** geometric sans (no Fraunces / Inter)
- **Signature:** oversized `$12` next to a clean tray cutout on an asymmetric split
- Hero budget: brand + one line + price + CTA + product
- Cards only on pricing (interaction) and booking form
- Motion: load rise, price pop, scroll reveals, button press
