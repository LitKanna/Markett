Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

YOLKO ("we", "us") runs getyolko.com to take egg pickup orders at Paddy's Markets Flemington. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Order details: name, mobile number, bundle, quantity, and pickup day you enter when booking.
  • Payment: if you pay online, Stripe processes your payment. We do not store card numbers.
  • Website usage: basic analytics from our hosting provider (page views, device type) to keep the site running.

How we use it

  • Confirm and prepare your order for pickup.
  • Contact you about your booking if needed.
  • Improve stock planning and service.

Who we share with

We only share data with services needed to run the business:

  • Stripe: online payments.
  • Cloudflare: website hosting and security.
  • Meta / Google: only if you arrive via an ad (standard ad platform analytics).

We do not sell your personal information.

How long we keep it

Order records are kept for up to 2 years for business and tax records, then deleted.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us using the mobile number on your order confirmation or via WhatsApp at the number shown on getyolko.com.
